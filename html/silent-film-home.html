Silent Films

Welcome to a didgital archive of silent films!

Previous Next

A silent film is filmed with no recorded sound, and most importantly no dialogue! The silent film era lasted from 1895 to 1936. Since there is no dialogue, expression, gestures and emotion was really important to make the film interesting.

Silent projected film emerged in the early decades of cinema and proceeded to dominate the entertainment industry well into the video age. The silent film era can be traced back to the 1800’s, where Thomas Edison and the Lumiere brothers began creating technology that would change the film world forever. By 1985 the invention of projection machines were known to be the phenomenon of the age, and the silent film era, lasting into the late 1930’s, had set standards that would influence the production of motion pictures forever.

What are people saying about silent films?

Quote

-Laurie R. King
